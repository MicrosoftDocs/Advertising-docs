# ManualCpmBiddingScheme Data Object - Campaign Management
Defines an object that represents the manual CPM bid strategy type.

With the ManualCpm (manual cost per thousand impressions) bid strategy, you set the highest amount that you'd like to pay per 1,000 viewed impressions. A viewed impression is counted when more than 50% of the ad pixels are in a userâ€™s view and the ad is viewed for at least one second.
