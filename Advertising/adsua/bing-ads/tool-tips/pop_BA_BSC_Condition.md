# Condition

**What it is:**  The condition of your item, as listed in your catalog. Valid options are:
- New
- Used
- Collectable
- Open Box
- Refurbished
- Remanufactured

**What you need to know:**  Only **New**  items are eligible to be displayed in your product ads.
